# GoTogether SMART Goal

File status: from proposal phase of April 2026.

S: The app shall promote genuine social encounters through local, playful challenges (city quests), interest matching, an AI assistant, and an interactive social map, thereby reducing loneliness across all age groups.
M: A click dummy with pseudo-map including challenges and corporate design. Full-stack software prototype of app infrastructure with responsive design, integrated map, and challenges.
A: Despite increasing digital connectivity through social media platforms, loneliness and lack of connection are prevalent. Superficial interactions, passive consumption, lack of local offerings, and insufficient support during life changes contribute to people feeling lonely despite online activity. There is a lack of solutions for deep social relationships and personal encounters that go beyond digital profiles.
R: Feasibility through existing open-source solutions (e.g., OpenStreetMap), collaboration in a SCRUM team with 8 members, and clear task distribution.
T: By project submission in July of the summer semester 2026.
