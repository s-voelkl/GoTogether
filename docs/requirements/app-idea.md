# GoTogether App Idea

File status: from proposal phase of April 2026.

**TL;DR: The app combines city quests, emotion- and interest-based matching, and an AI assistant to transform digital contacts into genuine encounters. Playful challenges, local events, and a social map facilitate spontaneous meetups, while gamification elements provide long-term engagement motivation. Through the social battery index and personalized recommendations, every activity remains tailored to users' individual social needs.**

1. **City Quests**
   Inspired by Pokémon Go. Focus on playable, local city challenges that encourage mutual discovery.
   - Local meetup points: Locations (parks, cafés, landmarks) with mini-games, meetups, and interactions
   - Micro-challenges for encounters, e.g., talking to other app users on-site,
     > Pokémon Go increases spontaneous social meetings ([Research](https://newsroom.niu.edu/more-than-a-game-niu-psychology-professor-measures-pokemon-gos-impact-on-belonging/))
   - Team raids for social activities: Collaboratively complete larger tasks, e.g., cleanup events, large park picnics, city-wide treasure hunts, photography challenges
   - Time-limited events, e.g., city festivals, holidays, special offers
   - Persistent teams: Semi-permanent assignment to one of several (2-5) groups based on characteristics
   - Level- or team-dependent challenges for beginners to advanced users

2. **Personality & Emotion-Based Matching**
   - Social Battery Index: How much energy for social interaction (Low energy = quiet 1:1 activities, high energy = group quests & events)
   - Shared interests (Emotional Needs Matching). Examples: dogs, hiking, art, cooking
     > Based on [Gamification studies and anti-loneliness apps](https://openhsu.ub.hsu-hh.de/entities/publication/15111). Younger users also benefit from virtual contacts, older users require more in-person interactions ([Study](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/pdf/10.1080/10447318.2025.2543994))

3. **AI Personal Assistant**
   - Motivation & activity suggestions for users based on location, interests, social battery index, etc.
   - Local recommendations for activities, events, groups (Advertising possible)
   - Support for personal development and progress, including empathetic coaching for socialization & reflection prompts
   - Strong safeguards for safety, privacy, ethical AI use. Ads only clearly labeled, no manipulative advertising, data protection as priority
4. **Gamification & Rewards**
   - Rewards (avatar decorations, skins, virtual pets, badges, level-ups), also AR-compatible
   - Digital currency for in-app purchases, local partner offers, charitable donations, coupons, cashback coupons, loot boxes, season passes. Digital currency also purchasable by advertisers as activity rewards
   - Real-value coupons for local businesses, cafés, events only. Support for local economy and genuine encounters. Cooperation with local businesses possible
   - Bonuses/milestones for regular activity, friendship building, challenges, and city missions
   - Participation verification via QR codes on-site or through encrypted exchange with other users, preventing [GPS spoofing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPS_spoofing)

5. **Social Map**
   Inspired by Pokémon Go, [Jagat](https://techcrunch.com/2023/12/15/jagat-location-based-social-network-focuses-on-real-life-connections-surpasses-10m-users/), and Ingress Prime. Interactive live map
   - Interactive real-time map
     - Close radius (approx. 50m): Other online users nearby
     - Extended radius (approx. 500m): Local events, challenges, meetup points, group activities, local offers (Advertising possible)
     - Large radius (approx. 3km): Recommendations for places/activities. AI-powered, advertising possible
   - Anonymity & Safety: Optional safe mode for complete anonymity. Optional visibility for friends. Safe spots (e.g., cafés) available. Profile verification possible
